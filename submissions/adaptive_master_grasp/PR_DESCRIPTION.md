## Project Name

Adaptive Master Grasp

## One Line

FF Master performs adaptive arbitrary-object pickup without drops or damage using force-limited tactile control.

## Robot Platform

FF Master humanoid asset from `assets/Master` as the real robot reference, plus an inspectable MuJoCo task bench representing the right-arm wrist/end-effector with four adaptive fingers and declared tactile/object sensors.

## Task Goal

Pick up varied everyday objects safely: fragile egg, glass cup, foam cube, metal can, slippery bottle, deformable pouch, tool handle, and irregular parcel. The controller must choose a grasp mode, regulate contact force, recover slip, lift, carry, and place while staying below the object's damage force.

## Core Features

- Arbitrary-object benchmark with randomized mass, friction, pose, and sensor noise.
- Adaptive grip modes: pinch, tripod, enveloping, and cradle.
- Closed-loop slip margin regulation.
- Damage-aware force limiting for fragile objects.
- FF Master asset compile audit.
- 10-check submission audit and generated evidence pack.
- Demo video generated by submitted code.
- Autonomous FF Master bottle pickup rollout gated by sensed alignment, contact count, slip safety, and stable grasp steps.
